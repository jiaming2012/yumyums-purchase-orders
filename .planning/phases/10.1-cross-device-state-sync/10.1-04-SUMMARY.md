## Accomplishments

- LamportClock class with persistent device_id in IndexedDB syncMeta store (version 2 upgrade)
- WebSocket client with exponential backoff, drains offline queue before catch-up on every reconnect
- visibilitychange handler reconnects or re-syncs when app comes back to foreground
- applyOp applies incoming SET_FIELD ops to FIELD_RESPONSES/DRAFT_RESPONSES and re-renders the field
- renderFieldResponse re-renders a single field in the runner view without full checklist re-render
- 409 conflict in api() returns `{_conflict, winner}` sentinel; autoSaveField reverts to winner value
